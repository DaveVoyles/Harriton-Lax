# 📉 Game Adjustments Playbook

## 📋 Situation: Offense is Stalling
- **Fix:** Move to a 1-4-1 to stretch the zone.
- **Fix:** Focus on [2-Man Game](../offense/mastering-the-2-man-game.md) from the wing.

## 📋 Situation: Defense is Sliding Early
- **Fix:** Use skip-passes to the backside pipe.
- **Fix:** Execute 'Mumbo' (Pick for the feeder).

## 📋 Situation: Opponent is Dominating FO
- **Fix:** Shift to a '10-Man Ride' to disrupt the clear.
- **Fix:** Wing play focus: pinch and pop recovery.
