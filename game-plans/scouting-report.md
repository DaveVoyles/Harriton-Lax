# 🥍 Opposition Scouting Report

**Video Reference:** [Opposition Scouting Reference Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-xpNBfHYKqA)

## 🎯 Overview

The Opposition Scouting Report provides a structured framework for analyzing upcoming opponents. It focuses on identifying key personnel, offensive and defensive tendencies, and special teams strategies to develop a focused game plan.

**Target Audience:** Coaching Staff
**Focus Area:** Strategic Preparation, Tactical Adjustments, Personnel Mapping

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **Personnel Identification:** Identifying the opponent's primary threats (dodgers, shooters, crease players) and their preferred hands/moves.
2. **System Tendencies:** Analyzing whether the opponent plays a settled offense, transition-heavy style, or a specific defensive zone.
3. **Statistical Indicators:** Tracking face-off percentages, clear success rates, and man-up efficiency to identify points of vulnerability.

## ⚙️ Tactical Setup

- **Offensive Analysis:** Mapping out the opponent's primary sets (e.g., 3-1-2, 2-2-2) and their go-to plays in EMO situations.
- **Defensive Analysis:** Identifying the opponent's slide package, goalie habits, and clearing patterns.
- **Special Teams:** Evaluating the opponent's face-off wings and their execution of dead-ball restarts.

## 🏃 Execution Principles

### Phase 1: Observation

1. Review game film to identify recurring patterns in opponent behavior.
2. Annotate specific clips that demonstrate key offensive and defensive habits.

### Phase 2: Game Plan Integration

1. Integrate scouting findings into the weekly practice schedule.
2. Develop specific 'scout team' looks to simulate opponent sets during 6v6 drills.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

- **Objectivity 🔣:** Base assessments on data and recurring film evidence, not just single plays.
- **Communication 🗣️:** Ensure all players understand the 'Top 3' keys to victory based on the scouting report.
- **Adaptability 📐:** Be prepared to adjust the game plan if the opponent deviates from identified tendencies.

## ✅ Outcomes

- Increased team preparedness for specific opponent threats.
- High-level execution of tactical adjustments during the game.
- Improved overall win probability through strategic preparation.

## 📋 Coaching

- Review the scouting report with the team at least 48 hours prior to game time.
- Use the report to assign specific defensive matchups for long-poles.
- Continuously update the report based on in-season performance and adjustments.
