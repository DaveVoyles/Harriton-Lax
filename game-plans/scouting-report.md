# 🥍 Opposition Scouting Report

**Video Reference:** [Opposition Scouting Reference Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-xpNBfHYKqA)

## 🎯 Overview

The Opposition Scouting Report provides a structured framework for analyzing upcoming opponents. It focuses on identifying key personnel, offensive and defensive tendencies, and special teams strategies to develop a focused game plan.

**Target Audience:** Coaching Staff
**Focus Area:** Strategic Preparation, Tactical Adjustments, Personnel Mapping

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **Personnel Identification:** Identifying the opponent's primary threats (dodgers, shooters, crease players) and their preferred hands/moves.
2. **System Tendencies:** Analyzing whether the opponent plays a settled offense, transition-heavy style, or a specific defensive zone.
3. **Statistical Indicators:** Tracking face-off percentages, clear success rates, and man-up efficiency to identify points of vulnerability.

## ⚙️ Tactical Setup

- **Offensive Analysis:** Mapping out the opponent's primary sets (e.g., 3-1-2, 2-2-2) and their go-to plays in EMO situations.
- **Defensive Analysis:** Identifying the opponent's slide package, goalie habits, and clearing patterns.
