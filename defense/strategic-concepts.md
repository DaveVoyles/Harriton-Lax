# 🎯 Strategic Concepts

## ⚔️ Key Personnel Roles

Identifying the archetypes required for our systems.

### 🏹 The Dodge Initiator (Offense)
- **Profile:** High-end split/roll dodge, elite vision.
- **Objective:** Draw the first slide to trigger rotation.

### 🛡️ The Communication Hub (Defense)
- **Profile:** High Lax IQ, vocal, physically imposing.
- **Objective:** Command the slide package and declare the 'Hot' and 'Two' players.

### 🧤 The Transition Specialist (Midfield)
- **Profile:** High-speed endurance, elite ground ball vacuum.
- **Objective:** Exploit the [West Genny](../drills/west-genny-drill.md) philosophy in live games.
