# 🛡️ Advanced Defense Systems

## 📋 10-Man Ride
- **🎯 Goal:** Force the goalie to be the primary ball carrier.
- **The Tracker:** One Midfielder forces the goalie to a sideline.
- **The Wall:** Three Attackmen line up at the midline.

## 🛡️ Backer Zone System
- **🎯 Goal:** Protect the 'hole' with a roaming defender.
- **The Backer:** A 'Rover' who slides to every dodge while the Flat-5 cover the perimeter.
