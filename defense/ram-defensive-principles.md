## 🛡️ Core Defense Rules

### 💡 Quick Reference

- **Communication:** Loud "Ball/Left/Right" calls at all times.
- **Posture:** Sticks up and active in passing lanes; maintain a "man-ball" relationship.
- **Rotation:** "Touch the hat" through the middle on all slides and recoveries.

---

## 🐏 The Ram Defense Set

### 📋 Highlights

- **Slide Package:** Specific slide triggers for the Ram set (typically crease or near-side adjacent).
- **Recoveries:** Immediate sprint to the "hole" (the middle) after a slide is triggered.
- **Second-Slide:** Identifying the "Two" and "Three" defenders to provide depth behind the primary slide.
