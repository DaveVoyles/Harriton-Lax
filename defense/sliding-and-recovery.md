# 🥍 Defensive Sliding and Recovery

**Video Reference:** [Defensive Sliding & Recovery Reference Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xEnD16D8zL0&t=289s)

## 🎯 Overview

Defensive sliding and recovery is the backbone of any successful team defense. This strategy focuses on the mechanics of providing support to a beaten on-ball defender and the subsequent rotations required to maintain defensive integrity and minimize high-percentage scoring opportunities.

**Target Audience:** Defense, Midfield
**Focus Area:** Communication, Footwork, Rotations, Timing

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **The Crease Slide:** The primary slide typically comes from the crease to disrupt the dodger's path to the goal.
2. **Adjacent Support:** Being 'hot' or 'two' to provide immediate help if the primary slide is bypassed.
3. **'Fire' & Recover:** The clear communication to trigger a slide and the immediate recovery of the original defender to the vacated area.

## ⚙️ Tactical Setup

- **Slide Package:** Determination of whether the slide comes from the crease, the coma, or adjacent defenders.
- **Support Tiers:** Establishing 'Hot' (first slide), 'Two' (backside fill), and 'Three' (recovery coverage).
- **Stance:** Maintaining a 'man-ball' relationship with hips open to see both the immediate threat and the defensive set.

## 🏃 Execution Principles

### Phase 1: The Slide (The 'Hot')

1. Identify the dodger's commitment and wait for the 'go' signal or clear beat.
2. Slide 'to the spot' (the dodger's hands) rather than where they are.

### Phase 2: The Fill (The 'Two')

1. Backside defenders immediately rotate to cover the crease vacated by the primary slider.
2. Maintain spatial awareness to prevent skip passes.

### Phase 3: The Recovery

1. The beaten defender sprints to the 'hole' (crease) rather than chasing the ball.
2. Communicate with the team to identify the open man and re-establish the defensive shell.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

- **Decisiveness 🔣:** Effective sliding requires zero hesitation. Commit fully to the slide or stay home.
- **Communication 🗣️:** "I've got ball!", "I'm hot!", "I'm two!", "Fire!", "Recover!"
- **Spacing 📐:** Maintain tight cohesion to minimize passing lanes during rotations.

## ✅ Desired Outcomes

- Neutralization of primary offensive dodgers.
- Coordinated response to defensive breakdowns.
- Minimal high-percentage shots from the crease.

## 📋 Coaching Points

- Drill the '4-man rotation' frequently to build muscle memory for slide-and-fill sequences.
- Emphasize the 'sprint to the hole' recovery path for on-ball defenders.
- Reward clear, loud communication during live 6v6 scenarios.
