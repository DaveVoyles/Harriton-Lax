## 🎯 Overview

Close defense is about more than just size; it's about leverage, positioning, and dictated play. This guide highlights the elite habits of top-tier close defensemen, focusing on defending the X, goal-line extended (GLE), and the crease.

**Target Audience:** Close Defensemen.
**Focus Area:** Goal-Line Defense, Crease Play, and Physicality.
