# 🧤 Goal Keeper Training

**🎯 Overview:** High-level training for goalies focusing on reaction time, footwork, and clearing accuracy.

## 🏋️ Skills & Stick Work
- [Elite Wall Ball Routine](../drills/stick-work/elite-wall-ball-routine.md) – Goalie-specific routine: top-hand only and cross-body saves.
- **Reaction Drills:** Use tennis balls for faster reaction training.

## 🏹 Save Mechanics
- **Step to the Ball:** Always step 45 degrees towards the shot.
- **Top Hand Primary:** The top hand drives the save; the bottom hand provides stability.
- **Eyes on the Pocket:** Track the ball all the way into the mesh.

## 🛡️ Clearing Logic
- **Immediate Release:** Clear to the wings before the attack can ride.
- **Quarterback Mentality:** Call out slides and offensive positioning during the clear.

## ✅ Outcomes
- Improved save percentage through better positional discipline.
- Reduced transition defensive time by initiating fast breaks correctly.
