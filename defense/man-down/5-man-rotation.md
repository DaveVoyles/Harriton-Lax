# 🥍 5-Man Rotation: Man-Down Defense (MDD) Principles

**Video Reference:** [5-Man Rotation & MDD Strategies](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OQN-m20q8Lw)

## 🎯 Overview

Man-down defense (MDD) requires elite communication, discipline, and "string" logic to compensate for being outnumbered. This strategy focuses on protecting the "house" (the middle of the field) and forcing low-percentage outside shots while maintaining tight rotational integrity.

**Target Audience:** Defense, SSDM, LSM
**Focus Area:** Rotations, Communication, Stick Positioning, "String" Logic

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **Protect the "House":** The priority is to prevent high-percentage shots from the crease and the "slot." Defensive players must stay compact.
2. **The "String" Effect:** When one defender moves to the ball, the others must move in unison as if connected by a string to fill the vacated space.
3. **Stick in In-Lanes:** Sticks must be up and active to disrupt skip passes and force the offense to pass around the perimeter.

## ⚙️ Tactical Setup

- **Box-and-One / Box-and-In:** Common formations where four players form a box around the crease while the fifth (the "In") covers the most dangerous offensive threat.
- **Compact Shell:** Keeping the formation tight to minimize passing lanes through the middle.
- **Personnel Roles:** Designating the "Floor General" (usually the goalie or lead defender) to call the slides.

## 🏃 Execution Principles

### Phase 1: Identifying the Ball

- Immediately identify where the ball is and call "Ball!"
- The closest defender approaches with a "short" breakdown, focusing on containment rather than the takeaway.

### Phase 2: The Rotation (The "Slide")

- As the ball moves, the defense must rotate as a unit.
- Defenders on the backside must "sink" to the crease to cover the open man.

### Phase 3: Communication & Reset

- Constant calls: "I'm hot!", "I'm two!", "Ball-side!", "Backside!"
- If a slide occurs, the original defender recovers to the "hole" immediately to re-establish the formation.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

- **Patience 🧘:** Do not "chase" the ball. Let the offense pass it around the perimeter; only engage fully when they enter the "impact zone."
- **Discipline ⚖️:** Avoid throwing checks that leave you out of position. Your body is your primary tool in MDD.
- **Efficiency 📐:** Move with purpose. Small, efficient steps are better than long, lunging sprints.

## ✅ Outcomes

- Neutralization of the EMO (Man-Up) scoring threat.
- Forced outside, low-percentage shots for the goalie to save.
- Improved defensive cohesion and trust between teammates.

## 📋 Coaching

- Drill the "4-man box" rotation repetitively without a ball to build muscle memory.
- Emphasize "Sticks up!" during every live MDD scenario.
- Reward defenders who maintain the formation over those who attempt "hero" plays.
