# 🥍 Drill Catalog

A comprehensive collection of high-intensity, video-referenced lacrosse drills designed to improve fundamentals, transition play, and scoring efficiency.

## 🏋️ Skills & Stick Work

- [Elite Wall Ball Routine](stick-work/elite-wall-ball-routine.md) – High-rep routine for soft hands and ambidexterity.
- [One-Handed Stick Handling](stick-work/one-handed-stick-handling.md) – Forearm strength and ball retention during contact.
- [Catching Mechanics](stick-work/catching-mechanics.md) – Soft hands, targets, and tracking.
- [Cradling Mechanics](stick-work/cradling-mechanics.md) – Ball security and carrying under pressure.
- [Ground Ball Mechanics](stick-work/ground-ball-mechanics.md) – Run-through scoop, protect, and exit.

## 🏹 Offensive Drills

- [Ultimate Scoring Drill](ultimate-scoring-drill.md) – Finishing in the 'Red Zone' with deception.
- [Elite Offensive Dodge & Shoot](elite-offensive-dodge-shoot.md) – Dodging from the wing with stick protection.

## 🛡️ Defensive Drills

- [Durkin Defensive Footwork](durkin-defensive-footwork.md) – Pro-level breakdown and lateral shuffle mechanics.
- [Salisbury Call Drill](salisbury-call-drill.md) – 6v6 slide and recovery communication.
- [GOALIE - Goalkeeper Mastery](../defense/goalie-mastery.md) – Stance, angle play, saves, and clearing.

## 🏃 Transition & Fast Breaks

- [West Genny Drill](west-genny-drill.md) – Classic continuous 3v2 fast break scenario.
- [3v2 to 4v3 Drill](3v2-to-4v3-drill.md) – Integrative transition logic and decision making.
