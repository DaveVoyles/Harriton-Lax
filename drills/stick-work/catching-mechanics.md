# 🎯 Catching Mechanics (Partner Passing)

**Video Reference:** [Catching Mechanics Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w8TsIB95aII)

🎯 **Overview**
Improve catching consistency by training soft hands, stick presentation, and eye discipline.

💡 **Concepts**

- Catching is an _absorption_ skill: give with the ball into the pocket.
- Show a target early (stick up/out), then “meet” the pass.
- Eyes follow the ball all the way into the mesh.

⚙️ **Setup**

- Pairs, 1 ball per pair.
- Start 10–12 yards apart; progress to 15–20.
- Optional: cones to mark passing lanes.

🗺️ **Visual Map**

```text
   A (pass)                                 B (catch)

   stick up/out  ----->  o  ----->  stick up/out
    (target)                          (target)

   Key: eyes track ball → “give” into pocket → stick to the box.
```

🏃 **Execution**

1. **Target + track (20 catches each)**
   - Stick up/out as a target.
   - Call “HERE!” before the pass.
2. **Soft-hands catches (20 catches each)**
   - As the ball hits the mesh, _give back_ 3–6 inches.
   - Immediately bring the stick into the body (“to the box”).
3. **Quick stick transfer (10 reps each)**
   - Catch → immediate return pass (no cradle), focus on clean catch first.
4. **On-the-move variation (down and back)**
   - Jog while passing; maintain target and eye discipline.

🧠 **Mindset**

- “Quiet head, quiet hands.” Don’t flinch or stab at the ball.

✅ **Outcomes**

- Fewer drops in traffic.
- Faster catch-to-pass decisions.
- Better ball security on the run.

📋 **Coaching**

- If players “stab” at the ball: slow the pace and exaggerate the give.
- If targets are late: require target shown before passer begins motion.
