# 🥍 Elite Wall Ball Routine

**Video Reference:** [Elite Wall Ball | First Class Lacrosse](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7U3CvElp0FU)

## 🎯 Overview

Wall ball is the single best way to improve stick skills. This routine focuses on high-repetition, game-like touches to build muscle memory and soft hands.

**Target Audience:** All Players.
**Focus Area:** Passing, Catching, and Hand Speed.

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **Soft Hands:** Giving with the stick when catching to prevent the ball from bouncing out.
2. **Target Practice:** Aiming for a specific brick or spot on the wall for every pass.
3. **Ambidexterity:** Equal reps for both right and left hands.
4. **Game Speed:** Moving your feet and throwing with pace, not just standing still.

## ⚙️ Drill Setup

- **Equipment:** A solid wall, stick, and a ball.
- **Distance:** 5-10 yards from the wall.
