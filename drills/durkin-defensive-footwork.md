# 🥍 Tucker Durkin Defensive Footwork Drill

**Video Reference:** [Tucker Durkin Defensive Footwork | First Class Lacrosse](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7YYHks-Vexs)

## 🎯 Overview

This drill, led by pro defenseman Tucker Durkin, focuses on the fundamental footwork required for elite on-ball defense. It emphasizes the transition from an approach to a lateral shuffle, maintaining balance and leverage against a dodger.

**Target Audience:** Defensemen and LSMs.
**Focus Area:** Footwork, Balance, and Lateral Movement.

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **The Breakdown:** Transitioning from a full sprint to short, choppy steps as you approach the ball carrier.
2. **Large-to-Small Steps:** Starting with big strides to close space, then narrowing them for maximum agility.
3. **Staying Topside:** Positioning your feet to take away the middle of the field and force the dodger to the alley.
4. **Hip Fluidity:** Being able to flip your hips instantly when the dodger changes direction.

## ⚙️ Drill Setup

- **Players:** 1 on 1 or individual lines.
- **Equipment:** 4 cones in a diamond or square pattern (approx. 5-10 yards apart).
- **Field Position:** Can be done anywhere, but ideally in the defensive box.

## 🏃 Execution Principles

### Phase 1: The Linear Approach
- Sprint from the baseline to the first cone.
- Break down into a defensive stance 1-2 yards before the cone.

### Phase 2: Lateral Shuffle
- Shuffle laterally to the second cone, keeping your stick out and active.
- Do not cross your feet; maintain a wide base.

### Phase 3: The Drop Step
- At the cone, execute a sharp drop step and retreat/shuffle to the next marker.
- Keep your chest up and eyes on the imaginary dodger's hips.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

- **Patience ⏱️:** Allow the footwork to do the work. Don't reach with your stick until your feet are set.
- **Balance ⚖️:** Keep your weight centered; avoid leaning forward over your toes.
- **Discipline 🛡️:** Focus on perfect form over speed during the initial reps.

## ✅ Desired Outcomes

- Improved closing speed and breakdown mechanics.
- Enhanced ability to stay 'in front' of dodgers without lunging.
- More efficient hip transitions during change of direction.

## 📋 Coaching Points

- **Check the Feet:** Ensure players aren't 'clicking' their heels together during the shuffle.
- **Stick Position:** Hands should be 'away' from the body, not tucked in.
- **Voice:** Encourage defenders to call out their positioning.
