# 🥍 Mastering the 2-Man Game

**Video Reference:** [Stanwick Brothers Two Man Game Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GrhHfQCv7GE)

## 🎯 Overview

The 2-man game (picks and screens) is the most effective way to force defensive communication errors and create odd-man advantages in settled offense. This strategy focuses on the timing, angles, and decision-making required to consistently beat both man-to-man and switching defenses.

**Target Audience:** Attackmen and Midfielders.
**Focus Area:** Spacing, Pick Timing, and Reading Defensive Rotations.

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **The Set-Up:** The ball carrier must "set up" their defender by dodging toward the pick to force the defender to trail or get stuck.
2. **Pick Angles:** The player setting the pick must have their feet set and at a perpendicular angle to the defender's path.
3. **Reading the Defense:** Identify switches, stays, or under-plays to determine the next move.

## ⚙️ Offensive Setup

- **Formation:** Typically run on the wings or behind the goal (at "X").
- **Personnel Roles:**
  - **Ball Carrier (Initiator):** Responsible for timing the dodge with the pick.
  - **Picker (Screener):** Responsible for setting a solid base and then "rolling" or "popping" to open space.

## 🏃 Execution Principles

### Phase 1: Initiation
- The ball carrier starts a shallow dodge toward the designated pick area.
- The picker approaches the defender's blind side.

### Phase 2: The Engagement
- The ball carrier "brushes shoulders" with the picker to leave no room for the defender to squeeze through.
- The picker remains stationary until the ball carrier passes.

### Phase 3: The Release
- **Roll:** The picker turns and seals their defender, moving toward the goal.
- **Pop:** The picker moves away from the goal into open space for a skip pass/shot.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

- **Patience ⏱️:** Don't rush the pick. Let it develop so the defender is fully committed.
- **Communication 🗣️:** The offense should use verbal or non-verbal cues to trigger the pick.
- **Spacing 📐:** Keep the other four offensive players wide to prevent defensive slides.

## ✅ Desired Outcomes

- Creating a high-percentage 2v1 advantage in a small area.
- Forcing defensive mismatches through switches.
- Easy layup goals through successful rolls to the crease.

## 📋 Coaching Points

- **Feet Set:** Ensure the picker's feet are stationary to avoid moving screen penalties.
- **Shoulder-to-Shoulder:** Correct ball carriers who leave too much space from the picker.
- **Eyes Up:** The ball carrier must look for the picker rolling as much as they look for their own shot.
