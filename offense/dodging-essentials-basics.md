# 🥍 Offensive Basics: Dodging Essentials

**Video Reference:** [10 Best Lacrosse Dodges to Make You Unguardable](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=upVjyyIUqqU)

## 🎯 Overview

Dodging is the primary way to create scoring opportunities in lacrosse. This guide breaks down the essential dodges every offensive player must master.

**Target Audience:** Attackmen and Midfielders.
**Focus Area:** Split Dodge, Roll Dodge, and Face Dodge.

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **Change of Speed:** The most important part of any dodge is the sudden acceleration.
2. **Change of Direction:** Explosive lateral movement to beat the defender's hips.
3. **Stick Protection:** Keeping the stick away from the defender throughout the move.
4. **Reading the Defender:** Identifying if the defender is playing topside or sagging off.

## ⚙️ Setup

- **Approach:** Approach the defender at a controlled speed to set up the move.
- **Distance:** Execute the dodge approximately one stick-length away from the defender.

## 🏃 Execution Principles

### Phase 1: The Setup
- Use a jab step or head fake to freeze the defender.

### Phase 2: The Move
- **Split Dodge:** Transfer the stick from one hand to the other while crossing the defender's face.
- **Roll Dodge:** Turn your back to the defender and roll around their pressure.

### Phase 3: The Explosion
- Sprint out of the dodge toward the goal.
- Square shoulders to the target immediately.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

- **Be Decisive 😤:** Once you commit to a dodge, go at 100% speed.
- **Find the Island 🗺️:** Always dodge toward the high-percentage shooting areas.

## ✅ Desired Outcomes

- Consistently beating 1v1 defenders.
- Forcing the defense to slide, creating open teammates.

## 📋 Coaching Points

- **Low Center of Gravity:** Stay low through the turn to maintain balance.
- **Hands Fast:** Hand transitions during split dodges must be lightning fast.
