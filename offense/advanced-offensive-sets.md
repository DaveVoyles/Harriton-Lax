# 🏹 Advanced Offensive Sets

## 📋 Off-Ball Pick Series (Mumbo)
- **🎯 Goal:** Confuse the defense with baseline and crease screens.
- **Pick the Picker:** The initial picker seals their man for a quick look after the first pass.

## 🏹 Invert Sets (Big-Little Game)
- **🎯 Goal:** Force long-poles to defend behind the cage (X).
- **Matchup Hunting:** Identify the weakest short-stick and clear the wings for a 1v1.
