# 🥍 Advanced Offensive Sets

**Video Reference:** [All the 2 Man Games in Lacrosse | POWLAX](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Z7yh68cbnQ)

## 🎯 Overview

Advanced offensive sets that go beyond basic formations. Covers off-ball pick series (Mumbo), invert concepts, and motion variations for exploiting specific defensive weaknesses.

**Target Audience:** Attack, Midfield | **Focus Area:** Strategy, Reads, Patience

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **Off-Ball Pick Series (Mumbo)** — Confuse the defense with baseline and crease screens. The initial picker seals their man for a quick look after the first pass.
2. **Invert Sets (Big-Little Game)** — Force long-poles to defend behind the cage (X). Identify the weakest short-stick and clear the wings for a 1v1.
3. **Motion Principles** — Continuous player movement creates confusion and forces defensive switches.
4. **Mismatch Hunting** — Identify the weakest defender and create isolation opportunities.

## ⚙️ Offensive Setup

- **Mumbo:** From 1-4-1 (picks set at crease level).
- **Invert:** From 2-2-2 (attackmen rotate to top, middies drop to X).
- **Motion:** From 1-3-2 (continuous rotation).

## 🏃 Execution

1. **Phase 1:** Identify the defensive weakness (film study + in-game reads).
2. **Phase 2:** Call the appropriate set (Mumbo for picks, Invert for matchup, Motion for confusion).
3. **Phase 3:** Execute with patience — these sets require 3-4 ball rotations to develop.
4. **Phase 4:** Take the shot when the defense breaks down.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

Advanced sets require discipline and patience. Don't force — let the play develop. Trust your teammates' movement.

## ✅ Desired Outcomes

- Players execute 2-3 advanced sets per game.
- Matchup advantages are exploited consistently.
- The offense has answers for any defensive look.

## 📋 Coaching Points

- Introduce one set at a time — master it before adding another.
- Walk-through before live reps.
- Film study is essential for these concepts.

---

_Note: Advanced sets are only effective when the basics are mastered first._
