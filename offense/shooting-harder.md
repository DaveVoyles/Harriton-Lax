
# 🚀 How to Shoot Harder

🎯 **Overview:** This guide breaks down the mechanics of shooting a lacrosse ball with maximum velocity, inspired by techniques from top professionals.

💡 **Concepts:** Power in shooting doesn't come from the arms alone; it's a full-body kinetic chain. The key is to use your hips and core to generate torque and transfer that energy through your arms and into the stick.

⚙️ **Setup:**
- **Grip:** Your top hand should be about halfway down the shaft, and your bottom hand at the butt-end. This creates leverage.
- **Stance:** Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, perpendicular to the goal. Your front shoulder should be pointing at your target.

🏃 **Execution:**
1.  **Wind-up:** Bring your stick back and high, away from your body. Your hands should be up and away from your helmet.
2.  **Hip Rotation:** Initiate the shot by rotating your hips and torso toward the target. This is where the power is generated.
3.  **Pull & Push:** As your hips rotate, pull with your bottom hand and push with your top hand. This creates a lever effect, whipping the stick head forward.
4.  **Follow-through:** Snap your wrists and follow through towards the target. A proper follow-through ensures accuracy and maximizes power.

🧠 **Mindset:**
- **Relax:** Tensing up will slow down your muscles. Stay loose and focus on a fluid motion.
- **Confidence:** Visualize the ball going into the net before you even shoot.

✅ **Outcomes:**
- Increased shot speed and power.
- Better accuracy due to improved mechanics.
- A more efficient and deceptive shot.

📋 **Coaching:**
- **Drill:** Have players practice shooting without a ball to focus purely on mechanics.
- **Feedback:** Watch for players who are using only their arms and encourage them to engage their core and hips.
- **Progression:** Start with stationary shots and then move to shooting on the run.
