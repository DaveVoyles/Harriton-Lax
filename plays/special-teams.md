# ⚔️ Special Teams Strategy

## 🏹 Man-Up Offense (EMO)

**🎯 Goal:** Force the rotation and finish on the backside pipe.

### 📋 Sets
- **3-3 Set:** Classic perimeter passing to stretch the box.
- **2-4 Set:** Inside-out focus with two players on the crease.

### 🧠 Key Concepts
- **The 'Extra' Pass:** Never shoot the first open look; the second one is usually better.
- **Draw and Dump:** Attack the seams to force a defender to commit.

---

## 🛡️ Man-Down Defense (MDD)

**🎯 Goal:** Protect the 'Heart' of the defense and limit high-percentage shots.

### 📋 Formations
- [Box-and-One Strategy](../defense/README.md) – Locking their best shooter while the 4-man box rotates.
- **Diamond Rotation:** Used against 3-3 sets to keep a 'Point' man high.

### 🧠 Key Concepts
- **Active Sticks:** Keep sticks in passing lanes, not on the man.
- **Backside Awareness:** The '2' and '3' slide must anticipate the skip pass.
