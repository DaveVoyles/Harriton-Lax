# 🤼 Face-off Wing Play

**🎯 Overview:** Strategic positioning and communication for the wing players during the face-off draw.

## ⚙️ Setup


- FO specialist at the center X.
- Two wing players (L/R) behind the wing lines.


## 🏃 Execution

1. **Declare the Call:** FO specialist calls 'Pinch' or 'Pop' based on their move.
2. **Wing Positioning:** Wings 'Cheat' towards the defensive or offensive side based on the program call.
3. **The Box Out:** Wings must prioritize body position over the ball if it is a loose ground ball situation.


### 🗺️ Visual Map

```text
        [OFFENSE]
           |
     (W)---|---(W)
           X (Center FO)
     (W)---|---(W)

```

## 🧠 Mindset


- **Be a Vacuum:** See ball, get ball. Do not wait for it to come to you.
- **Body First:** In a 50/50 ball, put your body between the opponent and the ball.

## ✅ Outcomes

- Increased possession percentage from the X.
- Strategic control of the midfield transition.
