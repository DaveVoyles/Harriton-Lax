# 🥍 Clearing Strategy & Execution

**Video Reference:** [Clearing & Breaking the Ride](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sX7_dwPEUHs)

## 🎯 Overview

Clearing is the tactical bridge between a successful defensive stop and an offensive possession. This strategy focuses on expanding the field, maintaining spacing, and using the goalie as the "quarterback" to bypass riding pressure.

**Target Audience:** Defense, Goalies, Midfield
**Focus Area:** Spacing, Decision Making, Transition Speed

## 💡 Key Concepts

1. **Expanding the Field:** Immediate sprint to the wings and end-line to force the riding team to cover maximum ground.
2. **The "Safe" Outlet:** Establishing high-percentage passing lanes (typically to the "corner" or the "breakout" midfielder).
3. **Goalie as the Hub:** The goalie must be comfortable handling the ball and scanning the field to find the open man.

## ⚙️ Tactical Setup

- **The Breakout:** Defenders sprint to "the spots" (Goal-line extended, or "GLE", and the offensive wings).
- **Midfield Rotation:** Midfielders flash to the "center-line" while one remains back as a safety valve.
- **The "Over" Option:** Using a cross-field pass to exploit riding teams that over-commit to one side.

## 🏃 Execution Principles

### Phase 1: The Save & Secure

- The goalie secures the ball and immediately shouts "CLEAR!" to trigger the breakout.
- Defenders look to their assignments before the attack can set up their ride.

### Phase 2: The Outlet

- Goalie scans from the ball-side wing to the far-side wing.
- If no immediate outlet is open, the goalie looks for a midfielder "flashing" to the middle of the field.

### Phase 3: Passing the Midline

- Transition players carry the ball across the midline with speed or look for a pass to an open attacker.
- Ensure one defender/midfield stays back to prevent a transition counter or "over-the-top" ride.

## 🧠 Strategic Mindset

- **Composure 🧘:** Do not panic under pressure. Use the "run or pass" decision tree.
- **Vision 👁️:** Scan the entire field, not just the nearest player.
- **Communication 🗣️:** "Clear!", "Over!", "Hole!", "Middle!"

## ✅ Desired Outcomes

- Efficient transition from defense to offense.
- Minimal turnovers in the defensive half.
- Exploiting riding teams with fast-break scoring opportunities.

## 📋 Coaching Points

- Drill "Goalie to Wing" outlets repeatedly with varying pressure.
- Emphasize the "sprint to the GLE" for close defenders to stretch the attack.
- Use the [West Genny](../drills/west-genny-drill.md) drill to build muscle memory for transition decision-making.
