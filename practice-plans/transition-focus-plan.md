# 📋 Practice Plan: Transition & Fast Breaks

**📅 Duration:** 120 Minutes
**🎯 Focus:** Decision making in uneven situations and clearing speed.

## ⏱️ Schedule Breakdown

### 🏃 Phase 1: Dynamic Warm-up (0:00 - 0:15)

- Line drills with focus on over-the-shoulder catches.
- Goalies: [Elite Wall Ball Routine](../drills/stick-work/elite-wall-ball-routine.md) variations.

### 🏋️ Phase 2: Technical Stickwork (0:15 - 0:35)

- [One-Handed Stick Handling](../drills/stick-work/one-handed-stick-handling.md) – Focus on protection during the clear.
- 4-Corner Passing: Emphasis on lead passes and catching in stride.

### 🧠 Phase 3: Tactical Transition (0:35 - 1:05)

- [3v2 to 4v3 Drill](../drills/3v2-to-4v3-drill.md) (15 mins) – Focus on the 'point man' decision.
- [West Genny Drill](../drills/west-genny-drill.md) (15 mins) – High-tempo continuous transition logic.

### ⚔️ Phase 4: Competitive Scrimmage (1:05 - 1:50)

- Full Field Clearing: Defense must clear against a 10-man ride.
- Transition 5v4s starting from a ground ball at the midline.

### ✅ Phase 5: Cool Down & Recap (1:50 - 2:00)

- Review [Defense Rules](../defense/defense-rules.md) for transition recovery.
- Stretching and equipment check.
