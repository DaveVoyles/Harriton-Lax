# 📋 Practice Plan: Defense Focus

**📅 Duration:** 120 Minutes
**🎯 Focus:** Team defense fundamentals — on-ball mechanics, sliding rotations, communication, and competitive defensive reps.

## ⏱️ Schedule Breakdown

### 🏃 Phase 1: Dynamic Warm-up & Defensive Footwork (0:00 - 0:15)

- **Lateral Shuffle Relay:** Two lines at GLE, players shuffle from pipe to pipe maintaining a low athletic stance. Emphasize choppy feet and hip positioning.
- **Drop Step & Sprint:** Players backpedal 5 yards, execute a 180-degree drop step, and sprint to the cone. Alternate left and right drop steps.
- **Mirror Drill (Pairs):** One partner moves freely in a 5x5 yard box; the other mirrors every movement while maintaining proper body position (hands up, feet active).
- **Goalies:** Light arc work — step to ball from center pipe, focusing on top-hand punch and foot alignment.

### 🏋️ Phase 2: Positional Defense Technique (0:15 - 0:35)

- **Approach Angles (10 min):** Working from the [Defensive Approach Angles](../defense/defensive-approach-angles.md) framework, defenders practice closing out on a stationary dodger at the wing, top-side, and X positions. Coach cues: "Inside hip, stick on hands, feet chopping."
- **V-Hold Drill (5 min):** Defenders pair up and practice the V-hold technique per [On-Ball Defense Mechanics](../defense/on-ball-defense-mechanics.md). Offensive player holds the ball in the triple-threat position; defender works to maintain a stick-length distance, mirroring hands and keeping hips square.
- **Breakdown Steps (5 min):** Full-speed approach into breakdown steps. Defender sprints from the crease to the ball carrier at the wing, breaks down into short choppy steps at 3 yards, and plays the hands. Rotate after each rep.

### 🧠 Phase 3: Team Defense — Sliding & Recovery (0:35 - 1:05)

- **[Salisbury Call Drill](../drills/salisbury-call-drill.md) (15 min):** Run the full 6v6 sliding drill. Defenders call "I'm 2!" or "I'm 3!" to establish slide packages. Focus on communication tempo — calls must happen BEFORE the dodge, not during.
- **[Durkin Defensive Footwork](../drills/durkin-defensive-footwork.md) (10 min):** Isolate the lateral footwork progressions from the Durkin framework. Integrate with live approach-and-recover reps against a dodger from top-center.
- **6v6 Shell Drill (5 min):** Half-speed walk-through of [Sliding and Recovery](../defense/sliding-and-recovery.md) principles. Offense moves the ball around the horn; defense adjusts positioning, slides on every dodge, and recovers to the "hot" man. No live dodging — this is about positioning and talk.

### ⚔️ Phase 4: Competitive Scrimmage — Defensive Emphasis (1:05 - 1:50)

- **6v6 Live Half-Field (30 min):** Full-speed settled situations. Defense earns a point for every caused turnover, save, or clear. Offense earns a point only for goals. First team to 5 wins. Losing team runs.
- **Defensive Restart Scenarios (15 min):** Coach blows the whistle at random moments — defense must immediately identify the ball, communicate the slide package, and reset. Simulate broken plays, overthrown passes recovered by offense, and ground ball scrums.
- **Communication Emphasis:** If ANY defender fails to make an audible call during a live rep, the entire defensive unit does 5 push-ups. Build the habit of constant talk: "Ball! Ball! Ball!", "I'm two!", "Check middle!", "Fire! Fire!"

### ✅ Phase 5: Cool Down & Recap (1:50 - 2:00)

- Light jog and team stretch circle.
- **Review:** Highlight 2–3 clips from practice (coach video) showing proper slides vs. blown rotations.
- **Homework:** Each defender identifies one on-ball mechanic to work on before next practice from [On-Ball Defense Mechanics](../defense/on-ball-defense-mechanics.md).
- Equipment check and break.
