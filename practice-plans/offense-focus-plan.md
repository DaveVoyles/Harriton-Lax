# 📋 Practice Plan: Offense Focus

**📅 Duration:** 120 Minutes
**🎯 Focus:** Individual offensive skills — dodging, shooting, 2-man game execution — building into settled 6v6 offensive sets.

## ⏱️ Schedule Breakdown

### 🏃 Phase 1: Dynamic Warm-up & Dodging Footwork (0:00 - 0:15)

- **High-Knee / Karaoke Ladders:** Two lines across the field. Focus on hip fluidity and quick directional changes that translate to dodging.
- **Split Dodge Footwork (No Ball):** Players line up at the restraining line. On the whistle, execute a split dodge at a cone, explode past, decelerate, and repeat. Emphasize the "sell and rip" — selling one direction with the feet before ripping the opposite way.
- **Roll Dodge Footwork (No Ball):** Same setup. Players approach the cone, plant the outside foot, roll tight to the cone (simulating a defender), and accelerate out. Keep the roll tight — no drifting.
- **Goalies:** Warm-up with rapid-fire tennis ball saves from 10 yards.

### 🏋️ Phase 2: Technical Stickwork — Shooting & Passing (0:15 - 0:35)

- **[4-Point Shooting](../drills/4-point-shooting.md) (12 min):** Rotate shooters through all four primary shooting positions — top right, top left, wing right, wing left. Emphasize the mechanics from [Shooting Mechanics Basics](../offense/shooting-mechanics-basics.md): hands away from the body, step toward the target, snap the wrists through the release.
- **Partner Quick-Stick Passing (8 min):** Partners stand 15 yards apart. Catch and release in one motion — no cradling. Alternate right-hand and left-hand passes every 10 reps. Finish with behind-the-back feeds to build creativity.

### 🧠 Phase 3: Tactical Offense — 2-Man Game & Sets (0:35 - 1:05)

- **[Mastering the 2-Man Game](../offense/mastering-the-2-man-game.md) — Pick & Roll Progression (15 min):**
  1. **Dry Reps (5 min):** No defense. Ball carrier and picker walk through the pick-and-roll at each position — top, wing, X. Picker sets feet, ball carrier reads the pick side, and rolls or pops.
  2. **Passive Defense (5 min):** Add a passive on-ball defender and a passive pick defender. Ball carrier reads the switch or hedge and makes the right decision — reject the pick, use the pick, or skip pass.
  3. **Live Reps (5 min):** Full speed 2v2. Offense must score or create a quality shot within 8 seconds.
- **Dodging Essentials Integration (10 min):** From [Dodging Essentials Basics](../offense/dodging-essentials-basics.md), run 1v1 reps from the wing. Each dodger must execute a different dodge type each rep — split, roll, face, or question-mark dodge. Defenders play honest but not over-aggressive.
- **5-Man Settled Motion (5 min):** Walk-through the base offensive set. Ball movement around the horn — skip passes, adjacent passes, and backdoor cuts. No dodging allowed; focus on spacing and timing.

### ⚔️ Phase 4: Competitive Scrimmage — Offensive Sets (1:05 - 1:50)

- **6v6 Half-Field Live (30 min):** Offense must run a set play before any dodging is allowed. Coach calls the play: "2-man top," "pick-and-roll X," or "motion." Defense plays honest man-to-man.
- **Shot Clock Pressure (10 min):** 30-second shot clock. Offense must generate a quality shot (shot on cage from inside 12 yards) or they lose the ball. Forces urgency and decision making.
- **Finishing Drill (5 min):** Rapid-fire. Two lines, one at each wing. Player catches a feed from X and must shoot in one motion — time and room only. Goalies get live reps. Focus on placement: low-to-low, off-hip, high-to-high.

### ✅ Phase 5: Cool Down & Recap (1:50 - 2:00)

- Light jog and team stretch.
- **Key Takeaway Discussion:** "What makes a quality shot vs. a forced shot?" Reinforce that assists matter as much as goals.
- **Homework:** 50 wall ball reps each hand before next practice. Review [Shooting Mechanics Basics](../offense/shooting-mechanics-basics.md) for hand positioning cues.
- Equipment check and break.
